Life changes significantly due to random events. Let us be humble yet possess an open imagination with many possibilities in mind.

If we are well-prepared, responsive, and have courage, shouldn't we entrust our lives to chance? Amid uncertainty and chaos, sometimes a story can have more power than logic, and courage may be more necessary than knowledge.

The experiences of hardship leave scars that, even after recovery, forever alter our perspectives on risk, reward, and goals. Last year, I faced significant hardships. An accident affected my vision, and various personal issues compounded to make it a truly challenging time.

Let's think about what will not change. Bezos mentioned that the desire for low prices and fast delivery does not change. In work, family, and romantic relationships, what remains constant?
